Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

No two aglio e olio recipes are alike, but this one is pretty true to the classic method.

This is a traditional recipies from Naples.

The key is slowly toasting the garlic slices to a perfect golden brown in the olive oil.

If it's too light, you don't get the full flavor and if it's too dark it gets bitter.

Ingredients

You likely already have most of the ingredients you’ll need for this recipe on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Spaghetti: This recipe, which makes about four servings, starts with one pound of uncooked spaghetti.
  • Oil: You’ll need half a cup of olive oil.
  • Garlic: Six cloves of thinly sliced garlic lends bold flavor.
  • Seasonings: This recipe is flavored with crushed red pepper flakes, black pepper, and fresh parsley.
